1.  If you were to describe semantic HTML to the next cohort of students, what would you say?

Answer - Organizing content to indicate what properties to give to each group of content.


2.  Name two big differences between display: block; and display: inline;.

Answer -  The two big differences are, block has some whitespace above and below and it does not allow other HTML elements to be next to it, unless specifically coded to.(on separate line)


3.  What are the 4 areas of the box model?

Answer - The 4 areas of the box model are, margin, border, padding and content.


4.  While using flexbox, what axis does the following property work on: align-items: center?

Answer - The cross axis.
